Learn the Basics First

Mobile development is challenging, but there are many free courses out there. You will quickly pick up the basics and design apps to get familiar with the technology they are using. You can also find books about mobile development, which can give tips and new concepts.

Enroll in a Bootcamp

Bootcamps teach students many skills in a short amount of time and become experts by appling their skills in creating various mobile apps. There are many online bootcamps available, including Coding Dojo.

Choose a Platform

There are many platforms that are part of mobile development, such as iOS and Android. You have to choose a specific platform to create mobile apps, because each platform requires different skills and languages. It also makes it easier to specifically find what you need to learn.

Learn Programming Languages

No matter what platform you choose, they require a specific set of programming languages. These languages control your mobile app, and are used for different platforms. Click here to learn about the languages used for mobile development.

Build a Portfolio

There are many projects you can build for mobile development. One of the best ways to get better is to build projects to apply your skills. These projects are essential for building your portfolio and showcasing your skills in an interview resume.
